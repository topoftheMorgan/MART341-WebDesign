About Me

Morgan Bowman




My name is Morgan Bowman. I have been sewing for 16 years. When I was a kid, I loved stuffed animals and had so many. When I started discovering stuffed animal patterns on Pinterest, my mom was not to thrilled with me making even more stuffed animals.

At some point, a local shop owner saw my animals at the fair and told me I could easily sell these. I decided to give it a shot and set up my first booth at my local Farmers Market in the middle of the 2019 summer. I did pretty well so I expanded to winter and Christmas markets. My booth continued to grow over the next few years and now I can barely go an out of town market without 40 or more animals.
